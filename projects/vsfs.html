Heuristic Evaluation

I was approached by the Virtual Student Foreign Service to do some heuristic evaluation for their new micro-volunteering platform for students. My team and I created a specific set of heuristics based on the original set by Nielsen. We evaluated each section of the site over a span of two days and documented the entire process

Software Testing

We also did some usability testing of their software platform and found bugs and snags in their system. We reported the problems we found to the main third-party developers of the platform which they used to improve their system.

Report Compilation

To show our results and our evaluation to the client, I compiled everything into a report which we submitted formally to the VSFS. The response was positive and the VSFS was more than satisfied with the report and the results.
