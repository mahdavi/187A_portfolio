Projects 2011-2012

Virtual Student Foreign Service


In December of 2011, The Department of State eDiplomacy office released a new virtual service for students to microvolunteer for different embassies all over the world. I was approached to do basic usability testing and a heuristic evalutation using a modified version of Nielsen's heuristics.
