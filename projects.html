Projects 2011-2012

Virtual Student Foreign Service

In December of 2011, The Department of State eDiplomacy office released a new virtual service for students to micro-volunteer for different embassies all over the world. I was approached to do basic usability testing and a heuristic evaluation using a modified version of Nielsen's heuristics.
Click here for more info on the project.


Marc Jacobs: MarcFam

In November of 2011, the company I currently work for obtained a project from Marc Jacobs.
The client wanted to grab all the photos on twitter and instagram with the tags #MarcJacobsIntl, #MarcJacobs, or #MarcFam and post them on their site and have a gallery to view them all.
I designed the interactions and visual elements of the page.
Visit the project page.
